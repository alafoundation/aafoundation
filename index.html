من نحن Who We Are

A trust between donors and those in need

Al-Amanah Foundation is dedicated to serving humanity in the spirit of the Islamic values of compassion, justice, and Amanah — trust. We exist so that every act of Zakat, Sadaqah, and Waqf reaches the people who need it most, with dignity intact.

Every rial, dollar and dirham entrusted to us is treated as exactly that — an amanah. We publish where funds go, work with vetted local partners, and measure our programs against long-term outcomes, not just short-term relief.

Our Mission

To alleviate poverty, empower vulnerable communities, and create sustainable opportunities through Islamic charitable initiatives.

Our Vision

A world where every family has access to dignity, education, healthcare, food security, and opportunity.
